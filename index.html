Design is about communicating an idea. To be *a good designer, you have to *be a good communicator. That's why when I started at the University of South Carolina Aiken four years ago, I decided to get my degree in Communications with a minor in Studio Art. It has allowed me to build upon the design skills I already had while learning how to be a better communicator in the process.

In my free time while at University, I've designed a couple of books, websites and the look and feel of a conference, among other things. In all of my work, I've tried my best to communication to people through simplicity. It is the hallmark of both my design and my philosophy.

If you like what you see, shoot me an email and lets work together!
